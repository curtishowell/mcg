Past Projects

Real Networks

Our team worked with the Rhapsody division to help establish entry strategy into the mobile application space. We analyzed relevant data and metrics relating to mobile carriers, as well as mobile operating systems, to determine which channels would be most beneficial for Rhapsody to enter. The analysis was done in 3 distinct stages, centering around the mobile competitive landscape, music and application consumption, and demographic indicators. We presented our findings to the executive at Rhapsody and delivered both a Powerpoint presentation and dynamic Excel workbook with our detailed findings.

Bartlett Tree Experts

We created a marketing plan for Bartlett with the goal of penetrating the Puget Sound commercial & residential markets. Our team performed extensive research and analysis into the best channels of entry, most lucrative geographic areas, and effective implementation of a marketing strategy.

Expeditors Intl.

Our team worked with the Human Resources division to analyze the root cause of turnover at the company. We performed correlation and regression analysis across several metrics and were able to determine which company programs and initiatives were increasing turnover and which were keeping employees happy. The team then researched into industry trends and practices to create recommendations on what the company could do keep employees motivated, as well as satisfied. Our final deliverable, in the form of a PowerPoint presentation, written report, and Excel spreadsheets, were given to the company to synthesize our findings.

Microsoft

We performed an extensive competitive analysis for Microsoft's Partner Program Technical Services division. The project scope included the assessment of defined competitors and focused on program structure, breadth of services, pricing tiers, and transparency of program offerings.
